Borrow a Book, Journal, or iPad

  • Set up or manage your borrowing account
  • Loan periods, renewals and recalls, and overdue fees
  • Stanford/UCSF reciprocal services

Technology Lending

  • Borrow laptops, iPads, and other multimedia equipment
 

Renew an Item

Keep your book, journal, or iPad a little longer.

  • Renew online or by calling (415) 476-2336
  • Renew your Interlibrary Loan request online, by email, or by calling (415) 476-8272
 

Request

If UCSF doesn't own an article, journal, or book that you need, you can request it for free.

  • Request an item from another library
  • Track the status of your request online, by email, or by calling (415) 476-8272

Book Delivery Between Campuses

  • Get a book delivered to/from Parnassus, Mission Bay, or SFGH

Document Express (fee-based)

  • Don't have time to come into the Library? Get UCSF-owned articles and book chapters by email.
